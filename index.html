About Me

I have five years of experience in Artificial Intelligence and intelligent optimization, with research focusing on Large Language Models, Operations Research, and AI for Optimization (AI4Opt). My work primarily focuses on the research and application of multimodal and omnimodal large models, leveraging them to develop Deep Research products for complex real-world tasks. I have led the development of two core products: Advanced Planning and Scheduling (APS) System — an intelligent scheduling and resource optimization platform for manufacturing; and Le-OPT — a large-scale AI4Optimization platform integrating machine learning and operations research techniques. My research has been published at top-tier international conferences, including ICLR 2024, ICLR 2025, and NeurIPS 2025.
